Fast & Furious RC Car Club

About Us

FFRCCC is a long-established RC racing club, hosting racers young and old, novice and experienced, and catering for electric RC cars on one of our expertly-designed indoor carpet track. The venue is set up with a full PA system and we use the latest Mylaps RC4 timing system.

What we race: At the FFRCCC we race electric cars only. The cars are 1/10th scale off-road, well-known brands like Associated, X-Ray, Schumacher and Yokomo. There are a number of different brands and routes you can take to get into RC car racing, but the best way is to come to club and talk to our members before you buy anything.

When we race: Racing is held every Sunday from 10am until approx. 5pm. We have a practise night on Wednesday evenings. We also host two multi-round championships each year. Every Sunday there are between 20 and 35 racers, making it one of the more popular clubs in Ireland. We have racers from 7 years old to a lot older.

Membership

Membership entitles to unlimited use of the track at weekends and during Wednesday training sessions. Members also get a 5% discount on all products in the shop. Do not hesitate, join us today!
